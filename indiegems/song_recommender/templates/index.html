Click here to get recommendations for songs that ACTUALLY sound similar.

How are the recommendations retrieved?

  • A total of 545 indie/alt rock songs are stored in a dataset
  • For a given song, three similary components are measured:
    • BPM difference
    • Key or tonality equality (if both songs are in the same key)
    • Lyric similarity (based on a vector representation)
  • All three components are given an equal weight, from which a final score is computed
